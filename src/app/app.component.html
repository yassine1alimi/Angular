About Us

CONSOMI TOUNSI

The Internet is the largest and richest data library, which is an interesting tool for accessing knowledge and an information search tool for anyone wishing to explore a subject in depth. Our project consists of the design and implementation of a dynamic website that allows us to manage a shop that exclusively sells Tunisian products, while ensuring a good user experience by facilitating access to the products as well as the purchase. Indeed, the user will be entitled to home delivery and after-sales service.

CONSOMI TOUNSI as its name indicates it is a web site, Tunisian which represents the products 100% Tunisian on Tunisia and also elsewhere. The main activities of CONSOMI TOUNSI are the sale and after-sales service of Tunisian brand products.
